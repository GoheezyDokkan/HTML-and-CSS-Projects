This is my favorite anime opening song: Kawaita Sakebi.

It is used for Season 0 of the widely popular anime known as Yu-Gi-Oh! Sadly this "Season 0" only ran for 27 episodes, after which Konami purchased the rights to the franchise from Toei Animation, which then transformed Yu-Gi-Oh! into something completely unrecognizable to fans of the original work.
