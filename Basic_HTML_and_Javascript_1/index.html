My top 10 anime list

This is my favorite anime to date: Code Geass, it's essentially
a what-if scenario where the British Empire never fell and
instead became a world superpower. The main character is
a prince of the empire who is exiled to Japan, which is
now called Area 11. He is given a power called Geass,
which allows him to command anyone to do anything he
wants. He uses this power to start a rebellion against
the empire and eventually take over the world.

This anime places #2 on my list: Kenichi - The Mightiest Disciple,
the story begins with the young, unremarkable Kenichi Shirahama
who is constantly bullied by his classmates and members of the
Koryou High School Karate Club. He is approached by a new student
named Miu Furinji, who turns out to be a powerful martial artist
and offers to train Kenichi in martial arts. Kenichi eventually
joins the Ryozanpaku dojo, a dojo that is home to some of the
most powerful martial artists in the world. Kenichi trains under
the masters of the dojo and eventually becomes a powerful martial
artist himself. Trained in various martial arts, possessing
a gentle and kind heart, Kenichi uses his training to protect
the weak and innocent from the many enemies he encounters.

This one ranks #3 on my list: The Misfit of Demon King Academy.
The story begins with the Demon King of Tyranny, Anos Voldigoad,
who during the war between humans and demons, sacrifices himself
to divide the different worlds with the ultimate barrier spell,
saving his people. 2000 years later, he is reincarnated as a
"hybrid" of a demon and a human, and attends the Demon King
Academy, where he is considered a misfit due to his immense
magical power and inhumanely low scores on the entrance exams.
He soon learns that the name of the Demon King of Tyranny is
Avos Dilhevia, and that no one believes he is the reincarnation
of the Demon King, despite his overwhelming power. He decides
to reclaim his former title and place as the Demon King,
and thus begins his journey.

Coming in at #4 is Assassination Classroom, this anime tells the
story of a class of misfits who are tasked with assassinating
their teacher, who is a powerful alien that has destroyed the
moon and threatens to destroy the earth in a year. The students
are given special weapons and abilities to help them in their
mission, but the teacher is a master of assassination himself,
and teaches the students how to be assassins while also teaching
them life lessons. The students eventually grow to love their
teacher and become attached to him, and the teacher also grows
to love his students. The students eventually learn that their
teacher is not the monster they thought he was, and that he
actually cares about them and wants them to succeed in life.

For #5, we have Kill la Kill. This masterpiece of an
anime tells the story of a world where clothing is
power, and the main character, Ryuko Matoi, is on a
mission to find her father's killer. She is given a
special uniform called a Kamui, which is a sentient
uniform that gives her immense power. She attends a
school called Honnouji Academy, which is ruled by the
student council president, Satsuki Kiryuin. Satsuki
rules the school with an iron fist, and Ryuko is
challenged by Satsuki to a duel. Ryuko eventually
learns that Satsuki knows the identity of her father's
killer. Ryuko and Satsuki eventually team up to take
down the true enemy, who is the CEO of a clothing
company called REVOCS, who is using clothing to
control the world.

At #6 we have the father of all anime: the Dragon Ball franchise!
Dragon Ball tells the story of Goku, a Saiyan (alien warrior race)
who was sent to earth as a baby to destroy it, but instead
became its greatest warrior and protector. He is joined by his
friends Bulma, Krillin, Yamcha, Tien, and Chiaotzu, and together
they go on many adventures and fight many enemies. Dragon Ball Z
takes place 5 years after the end of Dragon Ball, and tells the
story of Goku's son, Gohan, and his friends as they fight to
protect the earth from the evil Saiyan, Vegeta, and his partner,
Nappa, after that traveling to Planet Namek and facing off against
the emperor of the universe: Frieza. Following Frieza come the androids
and Cell, a bio-mechanical android created for the sole purpose of
destroying Goku, who his son Gohan defeats. Dragon Ball Z ends with
Goku and his friends facing off against the evil Majin Buu in a battle
to determine the fate of the entire universe. Dragon Ball Super takes
place 6 months after the end of Dragon Ball Z, and tells the story of
Goku and his friends as they face off against the God of Destruction,
Beerus, and later other universes' finest warrior in the Tournament of Power.
Dragon Ball GT takes place 10 years after the end of Dragon Ball Z, and
tells the story of Goku and his friends as they travel the universe in
search of the Black Star Dragon Balls, which are scattered across
the universe. After that, they face off against the evil Baby, who
has taken over the earth and turned everyone into his slaves. After
that, they face off against the evil Shadow Dragons, who are the
result of the overuse of the Dragon Balls, which Goku and friends defeat
by calling forth the miraculous Spirit Bomb technique.
